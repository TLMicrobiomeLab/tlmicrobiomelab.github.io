Meet the Team

The TLMRL is a diverse group of researchers dedicated to solving complex agricultural challenges through microbiome science.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Hooman Derakhshani

Principal Investigator & Lab Director

Bio: Dr. Derakhshani leads the TLMRL with a focus on decoding the complex interactions between livestock hosts and their resident microbiota.

Research Focus: Host-microbe interactions, microbiome therapeutics, multi-omics.
